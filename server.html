Server

Definition: A server is a computer or program that provides services or resources to other computers or programs, known as clients, over a network.

A server operates by receiving requests from clients and then responding with the requested data or services. It can host websites, store and manage data, or provide various other services over the network.

Servers are crucial for the functioning of the internet and intranets, as they enable the sharing of resources and information across multiple devices.
